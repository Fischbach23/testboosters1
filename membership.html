Pay Your Membership Dues

On behalf of the St. John's Athletic Boosters, we are excited to kick off our Athletic Booster Membership Drive. As a member, you will play a vital role in supporting our athletes, coaches, and the entire St. John's athletic program. Our athletic program offers students the opportunity to develop physically, mentally, and socially while competing in various sports. However, in order to provide the necessary resources and equipment to maintain this program, we rely on the generous support of individuals like you. The Athletic Boosters are instrumental in ensuring our athletes have the tools and resources they need to succeed both on and off the field. Membership Benefits: • Member's child eligible for booster scholarship as a senior (2029 grads must be 4 year member) • Supporting all athletes and teams • Invitations to booster meetings (3rd Monday of each month @ 7pm in Little Theater) Yearly Cost of Membership: $50 for a couple, $25 for single
